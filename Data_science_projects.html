500 richest people of the world 2021


A small Tableau dashboard to see some details of a dataset with information of the 500 richest people of the world. original data didn't include the gender of each person, so I made a short script in Python to exctract the first name of each person and calculate the most possible gender, using aq public API.
I wanted to see the gender and the nationality of the richest people of the world, in order to show the gender gap between men and women about this issue too.
